Chomko & Rosier aims to create interesting design projects that utilise technology, making and urban intervention. We offer consultancy, workshops and design through to fabrication and delivery. Contact us at info@chomkorosier.com.

The studio focuses on non-screen-based interactive work. We follow a site-specific and user-centred design process, often incorporating new technology. Our work has been recognized by the London Design Museum, and has been exhibited at the Sydney Opera House, Mobile Museum Hong Kong, Perugia Journalism Festival, and Nuit Blanche Montreal.
